MONSTA X

The group who birthed a whole generation of idols.

2015 - Present

Well respected worldwide artists, known as MONSTA X the 3rd gen Kpop group who are trendsetters within the kpop industry.

The timeline of MONSTA X

  • Debuted May 14, 2015 under Starship Entertainment.
  • MONSTA X was formed through a survival program called NO.MERCY.
  • Made their Japanese Debut in May 17, 2017 with Hero.
  • Went on their First World Tour in 2017 - Beautiful World Tour.
  • Had their 1st win on a music show on November 14, 2017 with Dramarama.
  • went on their second World Tour in 2018 - The Connect World Tour.
  • released their 1st song in English in 2018.
  • In 2019 Wonho officially left the group selflessly.
  • Shownu enlisted in 2021 in military.
